    ABOUT US...
    We are a young polish company, but a thriving one, with a great willingness to develop and self-improvement with every subsequent realization. We try to create modern, minimalist and aesthetically pleasing designs, matching the trend of the time.

    Our creative agency is divided into three groups:

    VERTCORE DESIGN

    The main department is dedicated to creating websites, 2D graphics (including motion design) and non-linear editing of films, music videos and series.

    VERTCORE DESIGN FOR TWITCH

    The department comprehensively supplies streamers on the Twitch. tv platform with graphical interfaces.

    DIGITITLES IMAGINE

    The department supplies the creators with the opening and end credits for feature films and series.

    SERVICES PROVIDED BY VERTCORE DESIGN


      2D computer graphics.

      2D motion design.

      Responsive websites from scratch (layout, HTML5 and CSS3 code) compliant with W3C standards using the latest technologies including jQuery, JavaScript with AJAX and Frameworks. (soon).

      Non-linear video editing up to native 4K resolution, including cutting, simple effects (mask effect, cameratracking) and preliminary color grading.
