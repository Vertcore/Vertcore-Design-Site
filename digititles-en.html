    SERVICES PROVIDED BY DIGITITLES IMAGINE


      Designing and creating opening title sequences.

      Creating and including subtitles.

      Designing and creating end credits/end cards sequences.

    To realize the above mentioned services we use professional software of the company Adobe®, including Adobe® Photoshop, Adobe® After Effects with Adobe® Media Encoder and the company's extensive package New BlueFX including a professional tool for the creation of end credits sequences Titler Pro 7.
    Stages of production:
    1.
    Pre-composition and initial credits in columns/animation of the initial credits sequence.
    2.
    Overlaying effects on credits (gradient, additional animations), font selection and final compositing.
    3.
    Fixes, final rendering, preview presentation, payment and shipping to the customer.
    Credits are made in Polish and English. Credits in other languages should be translated by the client.
